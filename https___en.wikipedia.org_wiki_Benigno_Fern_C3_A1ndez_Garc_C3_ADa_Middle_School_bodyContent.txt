Find sources: "Benigno Fernández García Middle School" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (May 2023) (Learn how and when to remove this template message) Middle school in Cayey, Puerto Rico The Benigno Fernández García Middle School is a public school that offers classes to students from 6th to 8th grade in Cayey, Puerto Rico.[1] Formerly, the school used to offer classes from 7th to 9th grade. The school has more than 500 students.[2] References[edit] ^ "Escuelas Publicas en Cayey, Puerto Rico | Escuelas de PR". www.escuelasdepr.com. ^ "Escuela Benigno Fernandez Garcia". Team Nutrition.
